Lesson 01

Talking About Your Morning

Gunakan pola sederhana ini untuk menceritakan kegiatan pagi.

Vocabulary

  • wake upbangun tidur
  • take a showermandi
  • have breakfastsarapan
  • leave for workberangkat kerja
  • arrive earlydatang lebih awal

Pattern

I usually + verb + at + time.

Contoh: I

usually wake up at 5 a.m.

Artinya: Saya biasanya bangun jam 5 pagi.

Practice

Short Conversation

<span>A: What time do you usually wake up?

B: I usually wake up at five thirty.

A: Do you have breakfast at home?

B: Yes, I have breakfast before I leave for work.

Tip: Baca dialog ini 3 kali: pelan, normal, lalu lebih cepat. Rekam suaramu dan bandingkan pengucapannya.
